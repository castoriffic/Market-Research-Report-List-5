<p><p>Oxidation Reduction Potential (ORP) electrodes are vital for monitoring chemical reactions involving electron transfer. The market is segmented into single junction and double junction types. Single junction electrodes have a single reference connection, making them simpler and cost-effective, but potentially vulnerable to contamination. In contrast, double junction electrodes feature two electrolyte chambers, providing enhanced protection against interference and longer lifespan, making them ideal for harsh conditions. Both types cater to different application needs within various industries, including water treatment and food safety.</p></p>
