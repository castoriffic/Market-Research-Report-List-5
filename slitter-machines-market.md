<p><p>Slitter machines come in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual slitter machines require operators to manually feed and adjust materials, making them ideal for small-scale operations. Semi-automatic slitter machines assist operators with material feeding and cutting, improving efficiency for medium-sized businesses. Fully automatic slitter machines are designed for high-volume production, requiring minimal operator intervention as they can feed, cut, and adjust materials automatically. Each type of slitter machine caters to different business needs based on scale and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
