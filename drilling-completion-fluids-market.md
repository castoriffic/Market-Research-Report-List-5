<p><p>Drilling and completion fluids are essential for the oil and gas industry, categorized into three main types. Water-based systems use water as the primary solvent, offering cost-effectiveness and environmental benefits. Oil-based systems, utilizing hydrocarbon fluids, provide superior stability and lubrication, making them ideal for challenging formations. Synthetic-based systems combine synthetic oils with water, balancing performance and ecological impact, often used in sensitive environments. Each type serves unique operational needs, optimizing drilling efficiency and well integrity.</p></p>
