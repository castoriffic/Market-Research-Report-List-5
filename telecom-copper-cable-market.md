<p><p>The Telecom Copper Cable market features two primary types: dry core and jelly-filled core cables. Dry core cables utilize a moisture-resistant design without additional filling, making them lightweight and easy to handle, ideal for indoor applications. In contrast, jelly-filled core cables contain a jelly-like substance that protects the conductors from moisture and environmental factors, making them well-suited for outdoor installations. Both types serve essential roles in telecommunications, addressing specific installation needs and environmental conditions effectively.</p></p>
