Microwave Semiconductors, on the other hand, are specifically designed to handle higher frequency signals in the microwave range, typically above 1 GHz. Both types of semiconductors play a crucial role in the telecommunications, aerospace, and defense industries, enabling the transmission of high-frequency signals for various applications such as wireless communication, radar systems, and satellite communication.</p></p>
