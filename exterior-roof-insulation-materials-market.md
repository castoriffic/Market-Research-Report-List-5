<p><p>Exterior roof insulation materials encompass various types, each offering unique advantages. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) and XPS (Extruded Polystyrene) are known for their durability and moisture resistance. Rock Wool provides excellent fire resistance and sound insulation, while PIR (Polyisocyanurate) and PU (Polyurethane) foam are favored for their high thermal efficiency. Glass Wool is lightweight and effective for sound absorption. Other materials include reflective insulation and fiber-based options, catering to diverse applications in residential, commercial, and industrial roofing projects.</p></p>
