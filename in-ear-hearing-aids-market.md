<p><p>In-ear hearing aids encompass various types designed to fit within the ear canal, including ITE full shell, half shell, and low shell models. The ITE full shell covers the entire concha, providing maximum amplification and battery life. The half shell occupies a portion of the concha, offering a balance between discretion and performance. The low shell is even smaller, fitting mainly in the ear canal for a subtler appearance, making it suitable for individuals seeking minimal visibility while still needing hearing support.</p></p>
