In an Open Auction market, ad impressions are sold to the highest bidder in a public auction where multiple advertisers can participate. In an Invited Auction market, ad impressions are sold through a private auction where specific advertisers are invited to bid on the impressions. Both market types allow for efficient and targeted ad buying while maximizing revenue for publishers.</p></p>
