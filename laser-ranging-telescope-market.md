<p><p>A Laser Ranging Telescope is a sophisticated optical instrument that utilizes laser beams to measure distances to astronomical objects with high precision. This technology is instrumental in various applications, including geodesy, space exploration, and satellite navigation. It involves emitting a laser pulse toward a target, measuring the time it takes for the pulse to return, and calculating the distance based on the speed of light.</p><p>The Laser Ranging Telescope Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.1% during the forecast period. Innovations such as integration with other navigation systems and improvements in data processing capabilities are emerging trends enhancing the functionality and accuracy of Laser Ranging Telescopes.
