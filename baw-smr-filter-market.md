<p><p>The BAW SMR filter market includes several types, each optimized for specific applications. Lattice type filters feature a configuration that enhances performance and minimizes insertion loss. Stacked crystal filters consist of multiple resonators arranged in layers, improving compactness and efficiency. Coupled resonator filters leverage closely spaced resonators to enhance selectivity and bandwidth, making them suitable for advanced communication systems.</p></p>
