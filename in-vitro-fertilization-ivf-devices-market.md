<p><p>The In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF) devices market comprises several key types. Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) involves injecting a single sperm directly into an egg, enhancing fertilization success. Frozen Embryo Replacement (FER) allows the transfer of previously frozen embryos, offering flexibility and convenience for patients. Pre-implantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD) screens embryos for genetic disorders before implantation, ensuring healthier outcomes. Together, these techniques represent essential advancements in assisted reproductive technologies, improving the chances of successful pregnancies for couples facing infertility challenges.</p></p>
