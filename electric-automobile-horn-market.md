<p><p>The electric automobile horn market is primarily categorized into two types: flat shape and snail shape horns. Flat shape horns are designed to be compact and lightweight, making them ideal for space-constrained vehicles while often delivering a sharp, loud tone. Snail shape horns, characterized by their spiral design, typically produce a deeper, more resonant sound and are favored for their reliability and effective sound projection. Both types cater to diverse automotive requirements, balancing aesthetics with auditory performance.</p></p>
