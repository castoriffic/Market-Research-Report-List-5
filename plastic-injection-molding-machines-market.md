<p><p>Plastic injection molding machines can be classified into three main types based on their power source: electric type, hydraulic type, and hybrid type. Electric type machines are energy-efficient and provide high levels of precision. Hydraulic type machines are well-suited for high-volume production and offer robust performance. Hybrid type machines combine the benefits of both electric and hydraulic systems, offering a balance between energy efficiency and high performance. Each type has its unique advantages and is suitable for different applications in the plastic manufacturing industry.</p></p>
