<p><p>Manual Transmission Fluid (MTF) is available in three main types: Mineral MTF, Synthetic MTF, and Semi-Synthetic MTF. Mineral MTF is made from refined crude oil, providing basic lubrication for older vehicles. Synthetic MTF is chemically engineered for superior performance and longevity, ideal for high-performance vehicles. Semi-Synthetic MTF combines the benefits of both mineral and synthetic fluids, offering a balance of performance and affordability. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences of vehicle owners.</p></p>
