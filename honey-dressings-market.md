<p><p>Non-adhesive honey dressings are liquid-based dressings that are spread or poured onto wounds without sticking to the skin. These dressings are ideal for superficial wounds and provide a moist environment for optimal healing. On the other hand, adhesive honey dressings are designed to stick to the wound bed, providing a protective barrier and promoting healing. These dressings are suitable for deep or irregularly shaped wounds where a non-adhesive dressing may not stay in place. Both types of honey dressings have their own benefits and are chosen based on the specific wound characteristics.</p></p>
