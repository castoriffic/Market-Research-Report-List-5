<p><p>Disposable bronchoscopes are available in two main types: rigid bronchoscopes and flexible bronchoscopes. Rigid bronchoscopes are used for procedures that require a more rigid and straight instrument, allowing for better access and control during bronchoscopy. Flexible bronchoscopes, on the other hand, are used for procedures that require a more flexible and maneuverable instrument, enabling access to hard-to-reach areas of the lungs. Both types offer the convenience of disposability, eliminating the need for reprocessing and reducing the risk of cross-contamination.</p></p>
