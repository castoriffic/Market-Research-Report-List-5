<p><p>Organic fertilizers can be classified into three main types: plant-based, animal-based, and mineral-based. Plant-based fertilizers are made from composted plant materials rich in nutrients. Animal-based fertilizers are derived from animal manure or byproducts, providing essential nutrients for plants to thrive. Mineral-based fertilizers are sourced from natural minerals such as rock phosphate or limestone, offering a wide range of nutrients for plant growth. Each type of organic fertilizer provides unique benefits and can be tailored to specific plant needs.</p></p>
