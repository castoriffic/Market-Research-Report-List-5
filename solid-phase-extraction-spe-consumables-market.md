<p><p>The Solid Phase Extraction (SPE) consumables market comprises several key types: SPE cartridges, SPE disks, and others. SPE cartridges are cylindrical containers packed with adsorbent materials, designed for liquid sample purification. SPE disks, on the other hand, offer a flat shape with similar adsorbent properties, providing higher surface area for efficient extraction. Other products may include SPE tubes and micro-extraction plates, catering to specific applications. Together, these consumables enable efficient sample preparation in analytical chemistry and environmental testing.</p></p>
