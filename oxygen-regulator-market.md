<p><p>Oxygen regulators are categorized into three main types: piston type, diaphragm type, and click style. </p><p>Piston type regulators use a piston mechanism to control the flow of oxygen, providing consistent and accurate pressure regulation. </p><p>Diaphragm type regulators utilize a flexible diaphragm to adjust the flow of oxygen. </p><p>Click style regulators have a simple adjustment mechanism that allows users to easily select the desired flow rate with a clicking motion. </p><p>Each type offers unique benefits and variations in performance for different medical applications.</p></p>
