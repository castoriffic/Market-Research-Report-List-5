<p><p>Tension clutches are commonly divided into three main types: electromagnetic tension clutch, friction tension clutch, and other variations. Electromagnetic tension clutches use electromagnetic force to engage and disengage the clutch. Friction tension clutches operate by creating friction between the clutch plates to transmit power. The "others" category includes variations such as pneumatic tension clutches or hydraulic tension clutches. Each type of tension clutch offers unique benefits and is suited to different applications based on factors such as torque requirements and operating conditions.</p></p>
