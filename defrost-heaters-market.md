<p><p>Defrost heaters are commonly used in refrigeration systems to prevent the buildup of ice and frost. There are three main types of defrost heaters: straight length, cartridge, and others. Straight length heaters are long rods that are usually made of stainless steel or copper. Cartridge heaters are compact and easy to install, consisting of a heating element enclosed in a metal sheath. Other types of defrost heaters may include wire heaters or flexible heaters, offering different shapes and sizes to suit various applications.</p></p>
