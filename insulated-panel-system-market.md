<p><p>Insulated panel systems are a popular choice for building construction due to their energy efficiency and ease of installation. The market for insulated panels can be divided into different types, including PU sandwich panels, EPS sandwich panels, and mineral wool sandwich panels. PU sandwich panels consist of a rigid polyurethane core sandwiched between two layers of metal, providing excellent insulation properties. EPS sandwich panels use expanded polystyrene as the core material, offering lightweight construction. Mineral wool sandwich panels utilize mineral fibers for insulation, providing fire resistance and acoustic properties. Other types of insulated panels may include PVC, PIR, and phenolic foam panels.</p></p>
