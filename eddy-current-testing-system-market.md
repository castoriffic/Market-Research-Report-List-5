<p><p>Eddy Current Testing (ECT) systems are categorized into small bandwidth and large bandwidth markets based on their frequency range and application. Small bandwidth systems operate at lower frequencies, ideal for detecting surface defects and permeability variations in conductive materials. They are commonly used in industries such as aerospace and automotive for quality assurance. In contrast, large bandwidth systems cover a broader frequency range, enabling deeper penetration and better detection of subsurface flaws. These are often used in heavy industries, including oil and gas, for comprehensive inspections.</p></p>
