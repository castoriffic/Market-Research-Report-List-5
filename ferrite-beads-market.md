<p><p>Ferrite beads are passive components used to suppress high-frequency noise in electronic circuits. The market is primarily divided into two types: ferrite chip beads and through-hole beads. Ferrite chip beads are compact, surface-mount devices ideal for small electronic applications, providing effective noise suppression in tight spaces. Through-hole beads, on the other hand, are larger and designed for traditional circuit boards, offering higher power handling and robustness in applications where size is less of a constraint.</p></p>
