<p><p>Infrared space heaters come in two main types: local heating space heaters and integral heating space heaters. Local heating space heaters are designed to heat a specific area or zone within a room, providing targeted warmth where needed. Integral heating space heaters, on the other hand, are built into a larger heating system, such as a fireplace or central heating system, and provide overall warmth to the entire space. Both types of heaters utilize infrared technology to efficiently and effectively heat a space.</p></p>
