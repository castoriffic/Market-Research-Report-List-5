<p><p>Automotive Around View Monitoring (AVM) is an advanced driver-assistance system (ADAS) technology that provides a 360-degree bird's-eye view of the vehicle's surroundings using multiple cameras. AVM systems enhance safety, reduce blind spots, and improve overall driving convenience.</p><p>The Automotive Around View Monitoring (AVM) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period. The integration of AVM with other ADAS technologies, such as blind-spot detection and parking assistance, is further fueling market expansion.</p><p>Key trends in the AVM market include the development of high-resolution cameras, AI-based image processing, and the integration of augmented reality (AR) for enhanced visualization. Emerging markets in Asia-Pacific and Latin America are also expected to witness significant adoption due to increasing vehicle production and rising consumer awareness about safety technologies.</p></p>
