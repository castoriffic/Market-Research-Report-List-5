<p><p>Ultrasound simulators are categorized into three main types based on the technology used: VR-based, AR-based, and MR-based. VR-based ultrasound simulators utilize virtual reality technology to create a realistic training environment for medical professionals to practice ultrasound procedures. AR-based simulators overlay computer-generated images onto the user's view of the real world, enhancing the training experience. MR-based simulators combine elements of both VR and AR technologies to provide an immersive and interactive training platform for ultrasound procedures.</p></p>
