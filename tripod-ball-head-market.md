<p><p>Tripod ball heads come in three main types: single action, double action, and triple action. Single action ball heads allow movement in one direction, usually just a panning motion. Double action ball heads enable movement in two directions, typically panning and tilting. Triple action ball heads offer movement in three directions, allowing for panning, tilting, and rotating. Each type offers varying degrees of flexibility and control, catering to different needs and preferences of photographers and videographers.</p></p>
