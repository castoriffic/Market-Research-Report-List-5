<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM) resins for medical instruments are classified into two main types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polymers consist of a single type of polymer chain, providing high strength, stiffness, and low friction, ideal for precision medical devices. Co-polymers, on the other hand, are formed from two different monomers, offering enhanced toughness and chemical resistance, which can be beneficial in more demanding applications. Both types are crucial for manufacturing high-quality, durable medical instruments.</p></p>
