<p><p>The robotic simulator market is categorized into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises simulators are installed and executed locally on users' hardware, offering enhanced data security and control but requiring significant upfront investments. Conversely, cloud-based simulators operate through internet connectivity, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs, as users access resources remotely. This model facilitates real-time collaboration and easier updates, making it ideal for businesses seeking quick deployment and reduced maintenance burdens.</p></p>
