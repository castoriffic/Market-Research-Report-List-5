<p><p>The compact photo printer market is segmented into two main types: no ink printing and sublimation print. No ink printing technology eliminates the need for traditional ink cartridges by using special paper that changes color when heat is applied. Sublimation print technology utilizes heat to transfer dye onto materials like paper, plastic, or fabric. Both types offer a convenient and mess-free way to produce high-quality prints quickly and efficiently, making them popular choices for on-the-go printing needs.</p></p>
