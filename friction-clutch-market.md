<p><ul><li>Single Disc Clutch</li><li>Double Disc Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Friction clutch market can be categorized into single disc clutch and double disc clutch. Single disc clutches have one friction disc that engages with the flywheel, providing moderate torque capacity and ease of use. On the other hand, double disc clutches have two friction discs which allow for higher torque capacity and faster gear shifts, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific needs of the vehicle or machinery.</p></p>
