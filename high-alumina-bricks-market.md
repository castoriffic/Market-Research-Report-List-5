<p><p>High Alumina Bricks are classified into two main types: Lining and Non-Lining. Lining High Alumina Bricks are specifically designed for use in high-temperature industrial furnaces and kilns as protective linings. They have excellent resistance to heat, abrasion, and chemical corrosion. Non-Lining High Alumina Bricks, on the other hand, are used for applications that do not require a protective lining, such as insulation or decorative purposes. Both types of High Alumina Bricks play a vital role in various industries, ensuring optimal performance and longevity of equipment.</p></p>
