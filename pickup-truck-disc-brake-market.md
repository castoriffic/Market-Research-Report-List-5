<p><strong>The Pickup Truck Disc Brake Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Opposed Piston</li><li>Floating Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pickup truck disc brake market includes two main types: opposed piston and floating caliper systems. Opposed piston brakes use multiple pistons on both sides of the rotor, providing balanced pressure and superior stopping power, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Floating caliper brakes have a single piston on one side, with the caliper sliding to apply pressure evenly, offering cost-effectiveness and simplicity. Both systems enhance braking performance, but opposed piston designs are more advanced, while floating calipers are widely used for their affordability and reliability.</p></p>
