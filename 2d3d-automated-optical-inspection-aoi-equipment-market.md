<p><p>The 2D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) equipment uses cameras to capture two-dimensional images of printed circuit boards (PCBs) and detects defects based on image comparisons, focusing on surface features. In contrast, 3D AOI equipment employs advanced imaging technology, such as laser triangulation, to create detailed three-dimensional representations of components. This enables more accurate detection of defects, including height variations and solder joint issues. Both types are essential for ensuring quality and reliability in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
