<p><p>Electrical room thermostats can be categorized into three main types: Millivolt Thermostats, 24 Volt Thermostats, and Line Voltage Thermostats. Millivolt thermostats are typically used in systems that do not have a power source and rely on a small voltage generated by the system itself. 24 Volt thermostats are commonly used in residential HVAC systems as they require a low voltage to operate. Line voltage thermostats are used in systems that operate on the standard voltage of the building's electrical system.</p></p>
