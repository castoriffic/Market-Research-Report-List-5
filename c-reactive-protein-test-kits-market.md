<p><ul><li>Immunoturbidimetric</li><li>ELISA</li><li>CLIA</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>C-Reactive Protein (CRP) test kits are categorized into several types based on their detection methods. Immunoturbidimetric kits measure light scattering caused by CRP-antibody complexes, providing rapid results. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) kits use enzyme-linked antibodies for high sensitivity and specificity, suitable for quantitative analysis. CLIA (Chemiluminescent Immunoassay) kits leverage luminescent reactions for enhanced accuracy and speed. Other types include lateral flow assays and colorimetric tests, catering to various clinical and research needs in assessing inflammation.</p></p>
