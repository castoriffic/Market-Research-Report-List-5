<p><p>TPU filament for 3D printing is categorized primarily into polyester-based and polyether-based types. Polyester-based TPU offers higher tensile strength, chemical resistance, and durability, making it ideal for applications like automotive parts and robust consumer goods. In contrast, polyether-based TPU is known for its flexibility, elasticity, and excellent resistance to hydrolysis, making it suitable for items like wearable devices and medical applications. Both types cater to different industrial needs, enhancing versatility in 3D printing solutions.</p></p>
