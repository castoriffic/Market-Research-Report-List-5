<p><p>The Bismaleimide (BMI) prepreg market is primarily segmented into unidirectional and fabric BMI prepregs. Unidirectional BMI prepreg features fibers aligned in a single direction, providing high tensile strength and stiffness, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Fabric BMI prepreg, on the other hand, consists of woven fibers, offering better drapability and impact resistance, making it suitable for complex shapes and structures. Both types are valued for their thermal resistance and chemical stability in high-performance applications.</p></p>
