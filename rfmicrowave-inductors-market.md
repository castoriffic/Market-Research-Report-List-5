<p><p>The RFMicrowave inductors market encompasses several types, including wire wound, film, and multilayer inductors. Wire wound inductors are constructed using insulated wire wrapped around a core, offering high inductance and low DC resistance. Film inductors utilize thin film technology for precision and compactness, providing excellent performance in high-frequency applications. Multilayer inductors feature multiple layers of conductive and insulating materials that enhance inductance density while minimizing size, making them ideal for modern electronic devices requiring efficient power management and signal integrity.</p></p>
