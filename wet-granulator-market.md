<p><strong>The Wet Granulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-shear</li><li>Low-shear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wet granulator market includes high-shear and low-shear types, catering to different industrial needs. High-shear granulators use intense mechanical force and rapid mixing to create dense, uniform granules, ideal for high-volume production and robust formulations. Low-shear granulators employ gentler mixing, suitable for heat-sensitive or fragile materials, ensuring minimal degradation. Both types utilize liquid binders to form granules but differ in energy input and application scope, making them essential for pharmaceutical, food, and chemical industries based on material properties and production requirements.</p></p>
