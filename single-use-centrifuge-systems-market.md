<p><p>The single-use centrifuge systems market is primarily categorized into high-speed single-use centrifuges and ultracentrifuge single-use centrifuges. High-speed single-use centrifuges are designed for rapid separation of various substances, commonly used in clinical and laboratory settings. In contrast, ultracentrifuge single-use centrifuges operate at extremely high speeds, enabling the separation of subcellular components and macromolecules, essential in biopharmaceutical applications. Both types offer the advantage of reduced cross-contamination risks and increased convenience, as they do not require extensive cleaning and maintenance.</p></p>
