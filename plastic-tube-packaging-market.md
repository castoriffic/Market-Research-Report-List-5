<p><p>Plastic tube packaging market is segmented into three major types: Polyethylene (PE), Polypropylene (PP), and Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC). PE tubes are known for their flexibility and durability, making them ideal for a wide range of products. PP tubes offer excellent chemical resistance and barrier properties, making them suitable for pharmaceutical and cosmetic applications. PVC tubes are known for their clarity and impact resistance, making them a popular choice for packaging cosmetics and personal care products. Each type of plastic tube offers unique benefits and applications in the packaging industry.</p></p>
