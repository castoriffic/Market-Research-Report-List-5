MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard) core doors are known for their smooth finish, durability, and resistance to warping, making them ideal for high-end applications. In contrast, particleboard core doors are more cost-effective, offering decent strength and stability, but may not be as resilient over time. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences, applications, and budget considerations, making them popular choices in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
