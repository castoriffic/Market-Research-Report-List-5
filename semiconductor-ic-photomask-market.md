<p><p>Semiconductor IC photomasks are essential tools in the production of integrated circuits. There are two main types of photomasks: Quartz masks and Soda masks. Quartz masks are made from high-purity quartz substrates and are known for their durability and high precision. They are commonly used in advanced semiconductor manufacturing processes. On the other hand, Soda masks are made from soda lime glass substrates and are more cost-effective but less durable than quartz masks. They are typically used in less advanced semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
