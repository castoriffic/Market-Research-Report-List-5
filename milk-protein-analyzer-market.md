<p><p>The milk protein analyzer market includes two primary types: ultrasonic milk analyzers and infrared milk analyzers. Ultrasonic milk analyzers use high-frequency sound waves to measure protein content and other components, providing rapid and accurate results. In contrast, infrared milk analyzers rely on light absorption characteristics to determine protein levels, offering a non-destructive and quick analysis. Both technologies serve the dairy industry by ensuring quality control and aiding in product formulation, catering to the growing demand for precise milk composition analysis.</p></p>
