Metal frame windows offer durability and excellent fire resistance, making them suitable for high-rise buildings. Wood frames provide a classic aesthetic and good insulation but may require more maintenance against fire regulations. Plastic frames, often made from PVC, are lightweight and resistant to corrosion but typically offer lower fire resistance compared to metal.
