<p><p>The Diagnostic Hearing Devices market includes various tools essential for assessing auditory function. Otoacoustic Emissions (OAE) devices evaluate cochlear function by measuring sounds produced in the inner ear. Audiometers assess hearing acuity across different frequencies. Otoscopes are used to visually inspect the ear canal and eardrum for abnormalities. Tympanometers test middle ear function and assess tympanic membrane mobility. Other devices may include specialized software and equipment for comprehensive hearing assessments, contributing to effective diagnosis and treatment of hearing disorders.</p></p>
