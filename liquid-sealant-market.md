<p><p>Liquid sealants are classified into four main types based on their composition: Resin Type, Rubber Type, Oil Modified Type, and Natural Polymer Type. Resin type sealants are made of synthetic resins that provide excellent adhesion and flexibility. Rubber type sealants are made of rubber elastomers that offer superior weather resistance. Oil modified sealants are composed of oils mixed with resins for improved durability. Natural polymer sealants are made from natural ingredients such as plant extracts, offering eco-friendly alternatives to traditional sealants.</p></p>
