<p><ul><li>Slide-type</li><li>Poppet-type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The slide-type reversing valve is a popular choice in the market due to its simplicity and cost-effectiveness. It consists of a sliding mechanism that redirects the flow of refrigerant to switch between heating and cooling modes. On the other hand, the poppet-type reversing valve offers better sealing performance and durability. It utilizes a poppet mechanism to control the direction of refrigerant flow, making it suitable for high-pressure applications. Both types of reversing valves play a crucial role in the HVAC industry for regulating the flow of refrigerant in heat pump systems.</p></p>
