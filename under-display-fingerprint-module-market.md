<p><strong>The Under display Fingerprint Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The under-display fingerprint module market consists of three main types: capacitive, optical, and ultrasonic fingerprint modules. Capacitive fingerprint modules use electrical signals to capture fingerprint patterns, offering quick and accurate recognition. Optical fingerprint modules utilize light to capture an image of the fingerprint, providing a reliable solution but may be affected by screen protectors. Ultrasonic fingerprint modules employ sound waves to create a 3D map of the fingerprint, delivering superior security and performance even in challenging conditions.</p></p>
