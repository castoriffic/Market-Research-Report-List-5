<p><strong>The Wireline Logging Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cased Hole Wireline Logging Services</li><li>Open Hole Wireline Logging Services</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireline logging services market is divided into two main types: cased hole and open hole wireline logging services. Cased hole services involve assessing wells that have been completed and are lined with casing, allowing for evaluation of the reservoir's characteristics and detecting issues like fluid migration. In contrast, open hole services are conducted in uncased wells, focusing on obtaining data about the formation's properties, porosity, and permeability before any casing installation, enabling effective reservoir evaluation and management.</p></p>
