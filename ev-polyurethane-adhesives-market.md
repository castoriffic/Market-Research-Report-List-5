<p><ul><li>Water-based Polyurethane Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-based Polyurethane Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EV Polyurethane Adhesives market comprises two main types: water-based and solvent-based polyurethane adhesives. Water-based polyurethane adhesives are environmentally friendly, featuring lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and providing excellent bonding properties for various applications, including automotive and electronics. In contrast, solvent-based polyurethane adhesives offer strong adhesion and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications but typically contain higher VOC levels. Both types cater to specific needs in industries such as construction, automotive, and packaging, emphasizing performance and environmental considerations.</p></p>
