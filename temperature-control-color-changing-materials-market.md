<p><p>Temperature control color changing materials are divided into two main types: reversible thermochromic materials and irreversible thermochromic materials. Reversible thermochromic materials change color temporarily in response to temperature fluctuations, making them ideal for applications like mood rings and thermometers. On the other hand, irreversible thermochromic materials permanently change color when exposed to specific temperatures, commonly used in packaging for indicating when a product has been exposed to heat or cold. These materials cater to various industries with their unique color-changing properties.</p></p>
