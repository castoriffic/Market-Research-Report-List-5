<p><p>Digital Printing Sublimation Ink Market Types are Direct Sublimation and Transfer Sublimation. Direct Sublimation involves printing directly onto the substrate, allowing the ink to sublimate and transfer color onto the material. This method is ideal for creating intricate designs on various substrates. Transfer Sublimation involves printing designs onto transfer paper and then transferring the design onto the substrate using heat and pressure. This method is commonly used for printing on fabrics and garments, providing vibrant and durable prints.</p></p>
