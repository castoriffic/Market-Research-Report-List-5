This type of device is commonly used for individuals with conductive or mixed hearing loss. </p><p>Bone Conduction Headphones, on the other hand, are non-invasive devices that rest on the bones surrounding the ear to transmit sound through vibrations. They are commonly used by individuals with hearing loss or for underwater activities.</p><p>Both devices offer alternative solutions for individuals with hearing impairments.</p></p>
