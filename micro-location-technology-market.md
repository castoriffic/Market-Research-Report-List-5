<p><p>Micro-location technology uses various technologies such as Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), Ultra-Wideband (UWB), Wi-Fi, and RFID to provide accurate location data within a small area. Wi-Fi can provide location information based on signal strength. RFID uses radio waves to track objects in real-time.
