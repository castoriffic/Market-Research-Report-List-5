<p><strong>The Hair Restoration Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Follicular Unit Extraction</li><li>Follicular Unit Transplantation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hair restoration market includes two main types: Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) and Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT). FUE involves extracting individual hair follicles from donor areas and implanting them into balding areas. FUT, also known as strip harvesting, involves removing a strip of skin with hair follicles from the donor area and transplanting it to the balding area. Both methods are effective in restoring hair, but FUE is less invasive and leaves minimal scarring compared to FUT.</p></p>
