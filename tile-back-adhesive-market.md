<p><p>The Tile Back Adhesive Market comprises three main types: 1 Component Paste BG, 1 Component Liquid BG, and 2 Component BG. The 1 Component Paste BG is a ready-to-use thick adhesive ideal for small to medium tile installations, offering high initial grab. The 1 Component Liquid BG is a more fluid option, suitable for large-scale applications and easier to apply. The 2 Component BG involves mixing a resin and hardener, providing superior bonding strength and flexibility for demanding environments.</p></p>
