<p><strong>The Food Grade Carrageenan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kappa Carrageenan</li><li>Lota Carrageenan</li><li>Lambda Carrageenan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food-grade carrageenan market comprises three main types: Kappa, Iota, and Lambda carrageenan. Kappa carrageenan forms strong gels in the presence of potassium ions, making it ideal for dairy products and desserts. Iota carrageenan, which forms softer gels with calcium ions, is often used in dairy and meat applications. Lambda carrageenan, known for its thickening and stabilizing properties, does not gel and is commonly found in products like sauces and soups. Each type caters to specific food formulation needs.</p></p>
