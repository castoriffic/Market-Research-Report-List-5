<p><p>The PTFE market is categorized into four main types: Granular, Fine Powder, Dispersion, and Micronized. Granular PTFE is used for applications requiring high crystallinity and strength, while Fine Powder PTFE offers enhanced flow properties for applications like coatings. Dispersion forms are used in liquid applications and coatings due to their ease of processing. Micronized PTFE is processed to a fine particle size, enhancing its performance in lubricants and specialized coatings, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
