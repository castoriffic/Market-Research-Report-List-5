<p><p>A Radon Mitigation Fan, also known as a radon fan or radon exhaust fan, is a device used to reduce radon levels in buildings by sucking air from beneath the foundation and venting it outside. Radon is a colorless, odorless, and tasteless radioactive gas that can seep into homes and other buildings from the soil. Prolonged exposure to high levels of radon has been linked to lung cancer.</p><p>The Radon Mitigation Fan Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period.
