<p><p>Furniture lacquer is commonly available in two types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based lacquers are known for their durability and fast drying time, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. However, they release harmful fumes and require proper ventilation during application. On the other hand, water-based lacquers are considered safer for both the environment and the user, as they have lower levels of volatile organic compounds. They also dry quickly and offer a more natural finish, making them a popular choice for eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>
