<p><p>Panel photoresist stripper is typically used in the semiconductor industry for the removal of photoresist coatings from panels during the manufacturing process. There are two main types of panel photoresist stripper available in the market: solvent-based and semi-aqueous. </p><p>Solvent-based panel photoresist strippers use organic solvents to dissolve and remove the photoresist, providing a high level of cleaning efficiency. On the other hand, semi-aqueous panel photoresist strippers use a mixture of water and organic solvents, offering a balance between performance and environmental considerations. Both types of panel photoresist strippers are widely used in the industry, with varying degrees of effectiveness and environmental impact.</p></p>
