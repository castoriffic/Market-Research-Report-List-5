<p><p>Bag compactors come in two main types: single chamber units and multi-chamber setups. On the other hand, multi-chamber setups consist of multiple chambers, allowing for the sorting and compacting of different types of waste materials. These are more suitable for larger facilities or areas where a variety of waste needs to be managed efficiently. Both types of bag compactors provide effective waste management solutions for different needs and spaces.</p></p>
