<p><p>Automation Light Grids are electro-sensitive light curtains which are used for perimeter guarding and presence detection in automation applications. These light grids consist of rows of light beams emitted from one side and received on the other side by photoelectric cells. When an object interrupts the beams, the light grid sends a signal to the control system to stop the machinery. </p><p>The Automation Light Grids market is witnessing steady growth due to increasing adoption of automation technology across various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and packaging.
