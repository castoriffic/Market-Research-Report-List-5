<p><strong>The Portable Hemostasis Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Detection</li><li>Mechanical Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Hemostasis Analyzers market is categorized into Optical Detection and Mechanical Detection types. Optical Detection analyzers utilize light-based technologies to measure blood coagulation by analyzing changes in light transmission or fluorescence as it passes through a sample. In contrast, Mechanical Detection analyzers assess hemostatic function through mechanical means, often employing shear stress or torque to evaluate blood coagulation properties. Each method offers unique advantages in portability, ease of use, and accuracy, catering to diverse clinical settings and requirements.</p></p>
