<p><p>Ophthalmology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) are essential tools in eye surgeries, utilizing electrical currents for cutting or coagulating tissue. The market primarily comprises three types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which use a single active electrode for treatments; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, featuring two electrodes that allow precise coagulation with minimal thermal damage; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed specifically for sealing blood vessels during surgery. Together, these units enhance surgical efficiency and patient outcomes in ophthalmologic procedures.</p></p>
