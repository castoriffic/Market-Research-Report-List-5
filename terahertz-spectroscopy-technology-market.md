<p><ul><li>Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy</li><li>Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Terahertz spectroscopy technology encompasses two primary market types: Terahertz Time Domain Spectroscopy (THz-TDS) and Terahertz Frequency Domain Spectroscopy (THz-FDS). THz-TDS measures the time-dependent electric field of terahertz pulses, allowing for high-resolution analysis of material properties, including solid, liquid, and gas phases. In contrast, THz-FDS involves the analysis of frequency-dependent responses, providing detailed insight into molecular vibrations and transitions. Both methods are essential for applications in material characterization, pharmaceuticals, and security, driving growth in the terahertz market.</p></p>
