<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mask Inspection/Review Equipment is essential in semiconductor manufacturing, primarily utilizing two methods: Die to Die (DD) and Die to Database (DB). The Die to Die method compares the features of a reference die with an inspected die, identifying defects through direct comparison. In contrast, the Die to Database method involves comparing the inspected die against a comprehensive database of known good die, ensuring that the features conform to established standards. Both methods enhance yield and production quality in the industry.</p></p>
