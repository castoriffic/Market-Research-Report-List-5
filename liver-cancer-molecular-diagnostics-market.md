DNA sequencing allows for the analysis of the entire genetic material of a cancer cell. Gene chip technology detects multiple genetic mutations simultaneously. These technologies play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring liver cancer, enabling personalized treatment plans for patients.</p></p>
