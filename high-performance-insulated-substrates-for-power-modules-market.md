<p><p>High Performance Insulated Substrates for Power Modules primarily consist of Aluminum Nitride (AlN) DBC (Direct Bonded Copper) substrates and Aluminum Oxide (Al2O3) DBC substrates. AlN DBC substrates offer superior thermal conductivity and electrical insulation, making them ideal for high-power applications where effective heat dissipation is crucial. In contrast, Al2O3 DBC substrates provide good insulation and lower thermal performance, suitable for less demanding applications. Both types enhance the reliability and efficiency of power electronic devices in various industries.</p></p>
