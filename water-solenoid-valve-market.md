<p><p>The water solenoid valve market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Straight Moving Type**: This valve type directly opens and closes under electric current, providing quick and reliable performance. </p><p>2. **Step By Step Straight Moving Type**: It operates in discrete steps, allowing for precise control over flow rates, useful in applications requiring variable flow control. </p><p>3. **Pilot Operated Type**: This design uses system pressure to control flow, enabling larger flow rates with lower power consumption, making it ideal for high-capacity applications. Each type offers unique advantages tailored to various operational needs.</p></p>
