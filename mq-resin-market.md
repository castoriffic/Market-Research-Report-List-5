<p><p>MQ resins are silicone-based polymers with unique properties. **Me-Silicone MQ resin** contains methyl groups, offering excellent heat resistance and water repellency. **Vi-Silicone MQ resin** incorporates vinyl groups, enhancing adhesion and cross-linking in silicone rubber. **Methyl Hydrogen MQ resin** features Si-H bonds, enabling curing and bonding applications. **Methyl Phenyl MQ resin** combines methyl and phenyl groups, providing improved thermal stability and flexibility. **Other MQ resins** include specialized formulations tailored for niche applications like coatings, adhesives, and electronics, leveraging their versatility and performance.</p></p>
