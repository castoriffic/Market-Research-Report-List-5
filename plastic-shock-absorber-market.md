<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Mechanical</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic shock absorber market is segmented into three main types: hydraulic, mechanical, and pneumatic. Hydraulic shock absorbers utilize fluid dynamics to dampen movement, providing a smooth ride in vehicles and machinery. Mechanical shock absorbers rely on springs and other mechanical components to absorb energy and reduce vibrations. Pneumatic shock absorbers use compressed air to cushion impacts, offering lightweight solutions ideal for various applications. Each type serves distinct functions across automotive, industrial, and consumer markets, emphasizing customization and performance.</p></p>
