<p><p>The distance image sensors market is primarily divided into two types: distance linear image sensors and distance area image sensors. Distance linear image sensors capture images along a single line, making them suitable for applications like scanning and profiling, where high-resolution data along a line is essential. In contrast, distance area image sensors capture two-dimensional images, allowing for comprehensive scene analysis and measurement. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, robotics, and industrial automation, offering solutions for precise distance measurement and spatial analysis.</p></p>
