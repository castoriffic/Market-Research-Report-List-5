<p><p>Space-based solar power market consists of three main types: wireless power transmission, earth-based receivers (rectennas), and other space devices. Wireless power transmission involves beaming solar power from space to earth using microwaves or lasers. Earth-based receivers (rectennas) collect and convert the transmitted energy into usable electricity. Other space devices refer to technologies such as solar panels installed on satellites to capture solar energy and transmit it back to earth. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing space-based solar power technology.</p></p>
