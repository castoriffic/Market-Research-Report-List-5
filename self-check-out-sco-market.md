<p><p>Self-Check-out (SCO) systems come in three main market types: Stand-alone, Wall-mounted, and Countertop. Stand-alone SCO systems are typically free-standing kiosks, offering customers a self-service option without needing to interact with store staff. Wall-mounted SCO systems are fixed to walls, saving space and allowing for a seamless integration into the store layout. Countertop SCO systems are smaller units placed on existing surfaces, providing a convenient self-checkout option without requiring additional floor space. Each market type caters to different store layouts and customer needs.</p></p>
