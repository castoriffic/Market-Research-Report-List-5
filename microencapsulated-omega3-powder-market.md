ALA is a plant-based omega-3 primarily found in flaxseeds and walnuts, while EPA and DHA are marine-based omega-3s found in fish oils and algae. ALA is often used in vegetarian and vegan products, whereas EPA and DHA are favored for their direct health benefits, such as reducing inflammation and supporting brain health.</p></p>
