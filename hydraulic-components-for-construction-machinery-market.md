<p><strong>The Hydraulic Components for Construction Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Element</li><li>Actuator</li><li>Control Element</li><li>Auxiliary Components</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic components for construction machinery market consists of several key types: </p><p>1. **Power Element**: Converts mechanical energy into hydraulic energy, typically through hydraulic pumps.</p><p>2. **Actuator**: Responsible for converting hydraulic energy back into mechanical movement, often seen in cylinders and motors.</p><p>3. **Control Element**: Manages the flow and direction of hydraulic fluid, including valves and pressure regulators.</p><p>4. **Auxiliary Components**: Support the hydraulic system, encompassing hoses, filters, and fittings, ensuring efficient operation and maintenance of machinery.</p></p>
