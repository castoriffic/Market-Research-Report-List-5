Pink particles are suitable for crops that require moderate amounts of chlorine, like rice and sugarcane. White particles are ideal for crops with low chlorine needs, such as wheat and corn. Each type of fertilizer is designed to meet the specific chlorine requirements of different crops, ensuring optimal growth and yield.</p></p>
