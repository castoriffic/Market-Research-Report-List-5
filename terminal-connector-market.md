<p><p>The terminal connector market can be categorized into several types, including screw type, spring-cage type, push-in type, and others. Screw type connectors provide secure, adjustable connections using screws for clamping wires. Spring-cage connectors use spring mechanisms for fast, tool-free insertion, ensuring reliable contact. Push-in connectors allow wires to be inserted directly into the terminal, simplifying installation. Other types encompass various specialized connectors designed for unique applications, serving diverse industries such as electronics, automotive, and industrial automation.</p></p>
