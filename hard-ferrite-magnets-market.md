<p><ul><li>Sintered Ferrite Magnet</li><li>Bonded Ferrite Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hard ferrite magnets market is primarily categorized into sintered ferrite magnets and bonded ferrite magnets. Sintered ferrite magnets are produced by a process that involves pressing and sintering materials, resulting in high magnetic strength and durability, making them ideal for various industrial applications. In contrast, bonded ferrite magnets are made by combining ferrite powders with resins or polymers, allowing for complex shapes and sizes. They offer flexibility in production and are commonly used in consumer electronics and automotive applications.</p></p>
