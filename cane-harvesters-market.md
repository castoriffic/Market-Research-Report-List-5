<p><p>Cane harvesters are machines used to efficiently harvest sugarcane crops. There are two main types of cane harvesters: wheel harvesters and crawler harvesters. Wheel harvesters are equipped with wheels, making them ideal for flat and level terrain. Crawler harvesters, on the other hand, are designed with tracks for better traction on rough and sloped surfaces. Both types of cane harvesters are used in the agricultural industry to increase productivity and reduce manual labor during the harvest season.</p></p>
