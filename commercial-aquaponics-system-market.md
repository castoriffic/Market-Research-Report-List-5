<p><ul><li>Media Filled Growbeds (MFG)</li><li>Nutrient Film Technique (NFT)</li><li>Deep Water Culture (DWC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial aquaponics systems can be categorized into three main market types based on the growing method used: Media Filled Growbeds (MFG), Nutrient Film Technique (NFT), and Deep Water Culture (DWC). MFG systems involve growing plants in a media-filled container, NFT systems use a thin film of nutrient-rich water flowing over plant roots, and DWC systems involve suspending plant roots directly in nutrient-rich water. Each market type offers unique benefits and challenges for commercial aquaponics operations.</p></p>
