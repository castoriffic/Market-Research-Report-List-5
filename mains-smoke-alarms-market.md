<p><p>Mains smoke alarms market includes three main types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and combination smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms use light sensors to detect visible smoke particles, making them effective for smoldering fires. Ionization smoke alarms detect fast-burning fires by sensing invisible smoke particles. Combination smoke alarms combine both technologies for comprehensive fire detection. Each type has its own strengths and is suited for different types of fires, providing consumers with options to ensure their safety in the event of a fire.</p></p>
