<p><p>Energy Saving Solutions market types include Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT), Energy Performance Contracting (EPC), Energy Management Contracting (EMC), and EPC plus Construction (EPC+C). BOT involves a private entity financing, designing, building, and operating an energy-saving project, then transferring it to the client. EPC involves a contractor guaranteeing energy savings through implementing energy-efficient solutions. EMC involves a third party managing a client's energy usage to decrease costs. EPC+C combines EPC with construction services to efficiently deliver energy-saving solutions. Each market type offers unique ways for clients to save energy and reduce costs.</p></p>
