<p><p>Programmable timer switches are available in different types, two common types being DIN Rail Mount and Panel Mount. DIN Rail Mount timer switches are designed to be installed on a DIN rail inside an electrical panel, making them easy to integrate into existing systems. Panel Mount timer switches, on the other hand, are mounted directly on a control panel or enclosure. Both types offer the ability to program schedules for turning devices on and off automatically, providing convenience and efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
