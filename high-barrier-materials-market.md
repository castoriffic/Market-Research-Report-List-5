<p><p>The high barrier materials market includes various types, such as PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride), EVOH (Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol), and PEN (Polyethylene Naphthalate). PVDC is known for its excellent moisture and oxygen barrier properties, making it suitable for food packaging. EVOH provides superior barrier performance against gases and is often used in applications requiring high purity. PEN offers enhanced thermal stability and strength, making it ideal for packaging sensitive products and in electronics. Together, these materials play crucial roles in preserving product integrity and extending shelf life.</p></p>
