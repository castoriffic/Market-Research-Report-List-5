<p><ul><li>Back Grinding Tapes</li><li>Dicing Tapes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor tapes market primarily includes back grinding tapes and dicing tapes. Back grinding tapes are used during the manufacturing process to support and protect semiconductor wafers while grinding them to specific thicknesses. Dicing tapes, on the other hand, are utilized for the separation of individual die from a wafer after it has been processed. Both types of tapes are essential for ensuring the integrity and efficiency of semiconductor production, catering to different stages in the manufacturing workflow.</p></p>
