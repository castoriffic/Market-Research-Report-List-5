<p><p>The mill liner market is primarily divided into two types: metal mill liners and rubber mill liners. Metal mill liners, typically made from alloys, are known for their durability and strength, making them suitable for high-impact applications in mines and large-scale production. In contrast, rubber mill liners offer enhanced resistance to corrosion and are lighter, which can reduce energy costs. Both types serve to protect the mill structure and optimize grinding efficiency, catering to different operational needs and preferences in various industries.</p></p>
