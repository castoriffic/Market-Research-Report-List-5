<p><p>Cardiac Biomarker Diagnostic Test Kits market includes various types such as Troponin Test Kits, Myoglobin Test Kits, CK-MB Test Kits, BNP Test Kits, and others. Troponin Test Kits are used to measure levels of troponin proteins in the blood, indicating heart muscle damage. Myoglobin Test Kits detect myoglobin, a protein released into the bloodstream after heart muscle injury. CK-MB Test Kits measure creatine kinase levels, another indicator of heart damage. BNP Test Kits detect levels of B-type natriuretic peptide, a hormone released in response to heart failure. Other kits may measure different biomarkers associated with cardiac conditions.</p></p>
