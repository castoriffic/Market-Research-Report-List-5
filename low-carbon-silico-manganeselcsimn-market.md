<p><p>Low Carbon Silico Manganese (LCSiMn) is primarily produced through two main methods: the Electrosilicothermic Method and the Shaker Method. The Electrosilicothermic Method involves using an electric furnace to reduce the manganese oxide with silicon and carbon, while the Shaker Method involves mixing silicon, manganese oxide, and coke in a shaker and then reducing them in a submerged arc furnace. Other markets for LCSiMn include the use of other reduction methods such as aluminothermic or carbothermic processes.</p></p>
