<p><ul><li>Refracting Telescope Accessories</li><li>Reflector Telescope Accessories</li><li>Catadioptric Telescope Accessories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Telescope accessories market can be divided into three main types based on the type of telescope they are designed for. Refracting telescope accessories are designed for use with refractor telescopes, which use lenses to gather and focus light. Reflector telescope accessories are compatible with reflector telescopes that use mirrors to gather and focus light. Catadioptric telescope accessories work with catadioptric telescopes, which use a combination of lenses and mirrors to achieve a compact design and high magnification capabilities.</p></p>
