<p><p>The concrete admixtures construction chemicals market is primarily divided into two types: mineral admixtures and chemical admixtures. Mineral admixtures include materials like fly ash, silica fume, and blast furnace slag, which enhance concrete properties such as workability and durability. Chemical admixtures consist of various additives like plasticizers, retarders, and accelerators that modify the setting time, consistency, and strength of concrete. Together, these admixtures improve performance, reduce water usage, and increase the lifespan of concrete structures.</p></p>
