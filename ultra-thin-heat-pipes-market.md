<p><p>The ultra-thin heat pipe market comprises primarily two types: round heat pipes and flat heat pipes. Round heat pipes feature a cylindrical design, making them suitable for applications requiring efficient heat transfer in small spaces, such as electronics cooling. In contrast, flat heat pipes have a planar shape, allowing for more surface area contact and optimal thermal management in confined environments, like LED lighting or compact devices. Both types enhance thermal performance while enabling innovative, space-saving solutions in various industries.</p></p>
