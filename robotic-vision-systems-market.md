<p><p>Robotic vision systems encompass two main types: 2D vision systems and 3D vision systems. 2D vision systems capture flat images to analyze features such as shape, size, and orientation. They are commonly used in sorting and quality control applications. On the other hand, 3D vision systems provide depth perception and are able to accurately measure distances and detect shapes in three dimensions, making them ideal for applications such as bin picking and object manipulation in robotics. Both systems are vital components in the growing field of industrial automation.</p></p>
