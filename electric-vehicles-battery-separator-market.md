<p><p>Electric Vehicles Battery Separator Market Types include single-layer (PP, PE), bilayer (PP/PE, PP/PP), and trilayer (PP/PE/PP) separators. Single-layer separators are made of either polypropylene (PP) or polyethylene (PE) material. Bilayer separators consist of a combination of PP and PE or two layers of PP. Trilayer separators have three layers, with PP sandwiched between PE layers. These different types of separators offer varying levels of performance, durability, and safety for electric vehicle batteries.</p></p>
