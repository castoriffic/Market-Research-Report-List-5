<p><p>The Shielded Separable Connector market is categorized into two types: With Lightning Arrester and Without Lightning Arrester. Connectors with lightning arresters integrate surge protection, safeguarding electrical systems from lightning-induced voltage spikes, making them ideal for areas prone to storms. Those without lightning arresters are simpler, cost-effective solutions for environments with minimal surge risks. Both types ensure reliable power distribution and insulation, but the choice depends on the need for surge protection and operational conditions.</p></p>
