<p><p>Tripod jacks are a type of civil aircraft jacks that have three legs and are commonly used for supporting the aircraft during maintenance tasks. They provide stability and weight distribution, making them suitable for smaller aircraft. Axle jacks, on the other hand, have a single axle and are designed to lift heavier aircraft by placing the jack under the landing gear. These jacks are typically used for larger aircraft and are capable of supporting greater weight loads. Both types of jacks play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient maintenance operations for civil aircraft.</p></p>
