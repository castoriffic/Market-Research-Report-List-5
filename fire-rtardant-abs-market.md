<p><p>Fire Retardant ABS Market includes two main types: Non-halogen Type and Halogen Type. Non-halogen Type fire retardant ABS materials do not contain any halogen elements, making them environmentally friendly and less toxic when burnt. Halogen Type fire retardant ABS materials, on the other hand, contain halogen elements like chlorine or bromine, which can release harmful gases when burnt. Both types provide varying degrees of fire resistance and are used in applications where fire safety is a priority.</p></p>
