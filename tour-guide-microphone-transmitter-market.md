<p><p>Tour guide microphone transmitter market can be categorized into two main types: wire transmitter and wireless transmitter. Wire transmitters are connected to the microphone via a physical cable, providing a stable and reliable audio connection. Wireless transmitters, on the other hand, use radio frequency technology to transmit audio signals to a receiver without the need for cables, offering greater freedom of movement for the tour guide. Both types of transmitters have their own advantages and are suited for different tour guiding scenarios.</p></p>
