<p><p>The milk replacer market is primarily categorized into whey-based and skim-based formulations. Whey-based milk replacers utilize whey protein, offering high digestibility and a rich amino acid profile, making them ideal for young livestock, particularly in the dairy sector. Skim-based milk replacers, derived from skim milk, provide essential nutrients and fat content, supporting growth and health in calves. Both types cater to different nutritional needs and preferences, influencing their application in animal husbandry and affecting market dynamics.</p></p>
