<p><strong>The Garbage Disposals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Batch Feed</li><li>Continuous Feed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The garbage disposal market is primarily divided into two types: batch feed and continuous feed. Batch feed disposals require users to load waste into the unit and cover it before operation, ensuring safety and preventing accidental activation. In contrast, continuous feed disposals allow for food waste to be added continuously while the unit is running, offering convenience for ongoing food preparation. Both types cater to varied consumer preferences, with batch feed being safer and continuous feed providing ease of use during cooking.</p></p>
