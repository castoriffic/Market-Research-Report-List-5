<p><p>The frosted acrylic sheet market is divided into two main types: one-sided frosted and double-sided frosted sheets. One-sided frosted sheets feature a textured finish on only one side, making them ideal for applications where transparency is needed on the untextured side. In contrast, double-sided frosted sheets have a frosted finish on both sides, providing enhanced privacy and aesthetic appeal. Both types are commonly used in signage, displays, partitions, and architectural applications, offering a balance of light diffusion and visual obscurity.</p></p>
