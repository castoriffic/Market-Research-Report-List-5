<p><p>Machine vision objectives are essential components in machine vision systems, serving as lenses that focus light onto a sensor to capture images with precision. Spherical objectives are commonly used in machine vision applications, providing high image quality and simple design. On the other hand, aspheric objectives offer advanced correction for optical aberrations, ensuring superior imaging performance. Both types cater to different market segments based on requirements such as cost, image quality, and application complexity.</p></p>
