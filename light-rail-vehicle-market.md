<p><p>A Light Rail Vehicle (LRV) is a type of urban transit vehicle designed for passenger transport on light rail systems. These vehicles are electrically powered, energy-efficient, and operate on fixed tracks, often sharing road space with other traffic or running on dedicated tracks.
