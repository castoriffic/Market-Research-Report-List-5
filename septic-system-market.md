<p><p>The septic system market encompasses various types tailored to different site conditions and needs. Conventional gravity systems rely on natural flow for wastewater treatment, while conventional-pump systems utilize pumps to move effluent. Pressure manifold systems distribute wastewater under pressure to enhance treatment. Low-pressure pipe (LPP) systems allow for shallow installation, and drip disposal systems use small-diameter tubing for precise distribution. Other systems may include advanced treatment technologies or alternative methods suited for specific environmental constraints.</p></p>
