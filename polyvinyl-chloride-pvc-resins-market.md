<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) resins market comprises three primary types: suspension, emulsion, and blended PVC. Suspension PVC, produced by polymerizing vinyl chloride in a suspension medium, is widely used in pipes, fittings, and medical supplies due to its excellent processing properties. Emulsion PVC, created through polymerization in an aqueous medium, is preferred for applications like coatings and adhesives, offering flexibility and adhesion. Blended PVC combines various formulations to enhance performance characteristics, catering to diverse applications across multiple industries.</p></p>
