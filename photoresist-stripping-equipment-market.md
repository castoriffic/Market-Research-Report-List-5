<p><ul><li>Dry Process Equipment</li><li>Wet Process Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photoresist stripping equipment market is divided into two main types: dry process equipment and wet process equipment. Dry process equipment utilizes plasma or vapor to remove photoresist materials from wafers, offering precise control and reduced chemical usage. In contrast, wet process equipment employs chemical solutions to dissolve photoresist layers, making it suitable for larger-scale operations. Both methods play crucial roles in the semiconductor manufacturing industry, ensuring the effective removal of unwanted materials during the production process.</p></p>
