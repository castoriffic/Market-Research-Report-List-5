<p><p>The ambulatory arrhythmia monitoring device market is divided into two main types: external and implantable devices. External devices, such as Holter monitors and event monitors, are worn on the body to continuously track heart rhythms for a specified period. In contrast, implantable devices, like insertable cardiac monitors, are surgically placed under the skin for long-term monitoring. Both types aim to detect and diagnose arrhythmias, improving patient outcomes through timely intervention and ongoing observation of heart health.</p></p>
