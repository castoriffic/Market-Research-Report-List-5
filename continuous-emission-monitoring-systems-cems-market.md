The market for CEMS is primarily categorized into two types: Extractive CEMS and In-Situ CEMS. Extractive CEMS involves sampling emissions and analyzing them in a lab setup, while In-Situ CEMS measures emissions directly at the source using on-site instrumentation. Both types play crucial roles in environmental monitoring and pollution control.</p></p>
