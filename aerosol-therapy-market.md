<p><p>Aerosol therapy market consists of three main types of devices: Inhalers, Nebulizers, and Bronchodilators. Inhalers are portable devices that deliver medication directly to the lungs through a metered dose, making them convenient for use on-the-go. Nebulizers are larger devices that convert liquid medication into a fine mist for inhalation, usually used at home or in a clinical setting. Bronchodilators are medications delivered through inhalers or nebulizers that help relax and open the airways, making breathing easier for individuals with respiratory conditions like asthma or COPD.</p></p>
